Ranking Revenue
Rankings

Ranked list of every hotel in the selected market by total room revenue.

Key definitions
Rank
Position by total room revenue among filtered hotels.
YoY%
Year-over-year change in revenue vs. the same month 12 months ago.
How to use this
The momentum arrow shows whether a hotel is climbing or falling in rank.
Multi-select tip
Hold Ctrl (or Cmd on Mac) and click filter boxes to select multiple items at once.
Data source: Texas State Comptroller Hotel Occupancy Tax public filings.
