Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how RankMyHotel.net ("we," "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our service.

  • Payment information: Card details and billing address are collected and stored by Stripe, our payment processor. We do not store full card numbers; we only retain a Stripe customer reference.
  • Usage data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized information about how visitors use the site, including pages viewed, session duration, browser type, and approximate geographic location (city level).
  • Authentication data: Memberstack manages your login session and stores a session token in your browser's local storage.

  • To process payments and send receipts

How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with the following service providers, who handle data on our behalf:

  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription management
  • Memberstack — account authentication and member management
  • Google Analytics — anonymized usage analytics

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights.

Cookies and tracking

The service uses browser local storage to maintain your authenticated session. Google Analytics may set cookies to track aggregated, anonymized usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information. To exercise these rights, use the contact details in the Contact section below.

California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of any sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

EU/UK residents (GDPR): You have additional rights including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Data retention

We retain account information for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to comply with legal and accounting obligations. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

Data security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and reputable third-party providers (Stripe is PCI-DSS certified; Memberstack uses bank-level encryption). However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

The service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us so we can delete it.
